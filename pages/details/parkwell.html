Parkwell

A parking locator & lot reserve application

Parkwell Africa

Parkwell is efficiently making parking easy, quick and effortless for drivers, space owners and cities, reducing traffic congestion and carbon emission. Designed to make it easy to locate and find free parking lot space across crowded or populated areas of Africa like Lagos.

Goal

  • Fast and easy to use parking locator.

  • Online booking system.

  • Online payment and check in/out.

  • GPS direction system.

  • User friendly.

My role

I worked primarily at Parkwell as a back-end python developer creating models, views and logic to help in the filtration and organization of parking lots within populated areas of Africa, concentrating more on Lagos.

With my contribution, Parkwell achieved

  • A 16% increase in parking slots added per quarter.

  • Created a new UI design to fit the targeted audience.

  • Increase in search query speed.
