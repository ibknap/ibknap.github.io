Parkwell Africa

Parkwell is efficiently making parking easy, quick and effortless for drivers, space owners and cities; reducing traffic congestion and carbon emission. Designed to make it easy to locate and find free parking lots space across crowded or populated areas of africa like lagos.

Goal

  • Fast and easy to use parking locator.

  • Online booking system.

  • Online payment and check in/out.

  • GPS direction system.

  • User friendly.
