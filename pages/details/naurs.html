Naurs The Music, Fine Art & Fitness Studio

Naurs is a web, iOS and Android application that connect users from virtual to the physical studio, allowing them monitor and view classes from their comfort zone. Also providing an easy way to purchase classes and packages with the ticket based system.

Goal

  • Connect users to any of they studios from comfort.

  • Online session booking.

  • Online programs (classes) & package purchase.

  • Free trial.

  • Online live or recorded sessions.

  • Ticket and wallet system.

My role

At Naurs I worked as the lead project designer and software developer (web, iOS & android). Occasionally, with some help from the marketing team. On the beta we worked closely to get customer feedback, requirements and helped prioritize business goals and revenue models.
