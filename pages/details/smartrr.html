SmartRR

Smart Reporting and Referral (SMART RR) is a technology based mobile application that allows survivors,social workers and service providers to report and refer cases/incidents of GBV from their smart and basic phones. The tool was developed by Big Family 360 Foundation, a national non-governmental organization in Nigeria.

Goal

  • Report GBV incidents.

  • Easily locate the nearest GBV center near you.

  • Provide safe zone for reports with no identity disclosure.
